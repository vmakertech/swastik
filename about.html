About us

Vaidya Shri Pramod Kumar Jaiswal was born in an Indian Hindu family on 30th March, 1978 at Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. He stands fourth amongst his siblings. His father’s name is Lt. Shri Ishwar Chandra Jaiswal. He got retirement as a Manager at State Bank of India and his Mother Smt. Shakuntala Jaiswal is a Homemaker. His Grandfather named Lt. Shri Hira Lal Jaiswal lived in his hometown Zamania, Ghazipur, Uttar Pradesh.


Vaidya ji has completed his primary education from Bhartiya Shishu Mandir, Varanasi. He pursued his secondary education from Bengali Tola Government Intermediate College, Mirzapur (U.P. Board) in 1994. Vaidya ji has done his professional course BAMS being selected through CPMT from Lalithari Government Post-Graduate Medical College & Hospital, Pilibhit in First division in 2004.

He further persuade his Masters in Ayurveda from Himaliiya Ayurveda Post-Graduate College & Hospital, Dehradoon, Uttarakhand University grading ‘A’ in first Division. In the year 2004, he established “Swastik Ayurved Yoga Evam Manovikar Kendra Pvt. Ltd.” In Varanasi (U.P.) for providing treatment for all sorts of diseases to the patients.

Vaidya Shri Pramod Jaiswal ji has been venerated with “Kashi Navratna - 2010” by Shraddha Channel, A renowned channel of Varanasi (U.P.).
